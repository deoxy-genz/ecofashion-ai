Understanding Sustainability Scores

High (70-100)

Excellent Sustainability

Uses eco-friendly materials, ethical production, low carbon footprint, highly recyclable.

Medium (40-69)

Moderate Sustainability

Some sustainable practices, room for improvement in materials or production methods.

Low (0-39)

Poor Sustainability

High environmental impact, non-sustainable materials, limited recyclability.
